About Green Gold Fusion LLC - FZ

Putting Your Trust in Our Green Thumb

At Green Gold Fusion LLC - FZ, we bridge the gap between producers and global markets by delivering premium-quality agricultural products, fresh produce, and oils. Based in the vibrant Meydan Free Zone, Dubai, we specialize in importing top-tier goods from India and exporting them to regions with significant demand, including America, China, the Netherlands, Kenya, and beyond.

Our operations focus on the import of a diverse range of agricultural produce, fresh fruits and vegetables, edible oils, and non-edible oils, ensuring the highest quality and sustainability. Among our portfolio, Castor Oil stands out as our flagship product. Sourced from India, it is exported worldwide, catering to industrial and commercial needs across global markets.

Green Gold Fusion LLC - FZ is committed to excellence, fostering strong partnerships, and delivering products that meet the expectations of an ever-evolving global market. Our strategic location, dedicated team, and customer-centric approach make us a trusted name in the trading industry.
